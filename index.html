THE COLLECTIVE

Server Rules & Guidelines
01
General Conduct
Zero Tolerance
  • 1.1 Respect all players. Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, and toxicity will result in an immediate and permanent ban.
  • 1.2 No slurs or offensive language. Filtered words are filtered for a reason. Attempting to bypass the word filter is a bannable offense.
  • 1.3 No mic spam or chat spam. This includes playing music through your mic, repeated messages, or excessive use of TTS chat.
  • 1.4 Listen to staff. Staff decisions are final in-game. If you believe a decision was wrong, appeal on Discord — do not argue in-game.
  • 1.5 No advertising. Do not advertise other servers, communities, or services.
  • 1.6 English in public chats. Use English in OOC and advert. Other languages are fine in local/private RP.
02
Roleplay Standards
Core RP
  • 2.1 Random Deathmatch (RDM) is prohibited. You must have a valid roleplay reason to kill another player. Self-defense, raids, hits, and muggings with proper initiation are valid.
  • 2.2 New Life Rule (NLR). After dying, you forget everything from your previous life. Do not return to the area of your death or seek revenge for 3 minutes.
  • 2.3 Fear RP. Value your life. If you are outnumbered or held at gunpoint, comply with demands as if you are truly scared for your life.
  • 2.4 No metagaming. Do not use out-of-character information (Discord calls, stream sniping, OOC chat) to gain an in-character advantage.
  • 2.5 No FailRP. Play your role realistically. A police officer should not commit crimes. A medic should not refuse to heal. Stay in character.
  • 2.6 RP names are required. Use a realistic or creative roleplay name (max 24 characters). No offensive or celebrity names.
03
Raiding & Criminal Activity
Combat
  • 3.1 Raiding cooldowns. You may raid the same base once every 20 minutes. You may not raid the same player more than twice per hour.
  • 3.2 Announce raids. You must use /advert Raid before initiating a raid. Counter-raids do not require adverts.
  • 3.3 Mugging rules. You must give victims at least 10 seconds to comply. Maximum mug amount is $1,000. You must face your target and may not mug AFK players.
  • 3.4 No revenge killing. NLR applies. If you died during a raid, you cannot return to defend or attack.
  • 3.5 Hitman rules. Hits require a valid reason. Hit prices range from $300 – $25,000. You have 20 minutes to complete a hit once accepted.
  • 3.6 Bank raids. Bank raids are high-risk events. Police may use lethal force. 12.5% of stolen funds are lost on raid completion.
04
Building & Props
Building
  • 4.1 Prop limit is 100. Build responsibly. Excessive or intentionally laggy builds will be removed.
  • 4.2 No prop abuse. Prop climbing, prop killing, prop surfing, prop blocking, and prop spamming are all prohibited.
  • 4.3 Bases must be raidable. Every base must have a clear entrance. No invisible props, maze bases, or crouch-only entrances that require pixel-perfect movement.
  • 4.4 Fading doors. Fading doors must remain open for at least 2 seconds. Max 3 fading doors per entrance. Fading door abuse is bannable.
  • 4.5 Building sign. If you're actively building, put up a text screen. You cannot have valuables (printers, drugs) while building.
  • 4.6 Door limit: 10. Do not buy excessive doors to block off areas you aren't using.
05
Government & Law Enforcement
Governance
  • 5.1 Police must enforce laws fairly. Do not arrest without cause. Warrants require evidence. Wanted status lasts 5 minutes.
  • 5.2 Jail time is 2 minutes. Do not extend jail time beyond what the system allows.
  • 5.3 Mayor laws must be reasonable. No troll laws. Max 10 active laws. Laws cannot override server rules.
  • 5.4 Lockdowns. Lockdowns require a valid RP reason.. Cooldown between lockdowns is 30 minutes.
  • 5.5 Corruption is not allowed. Government officials may not participate in criminal activity, accept bribes, or abuse their position.
  • 5.6 Taxes. Default tax rate is 5%, with a max of 20%. Treasury resets on mayor death.
06
AFK & Disconnects
System
  • 6.1 AFK players will be demoted after 1 hour of inactivity. You will be marked as AFK after 5 minutes.
  • 6.2 Grace reconnect. If you disconnect, you have 8 minutes to reconnect and retain your job, inventory, and props.
07
Exploits & Cheating
Permanent Ban
  • 7.1 No cheats, hacks, or exploits. Any use of third-party software, scripts, or exploits will result in a permanent ban without appeal.
  • 7.2 No duplicating money or items. Exploiting bugs to duplicate is treated the same as cheating.
  • 7.3 Report exploits. If you find a bug or exploit, report it to staff immediately. Exploiting a known bug is bannable.
Staff reserve the right to punish for actions not explicitly listed here. Use common sense. If you have to ask "is this allowed?" — it probably isn't.

By playing on The Collective, you agree to follow these rules at all times.
